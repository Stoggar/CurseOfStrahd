# Stairway tumble

We decided to check out the wine-cellar. 
We're looking for a particular wine after all. 
In the cellar were 4 druids. 
Instead of fighting them there, we drew them up the stairs and Brie toppled a wine-rack so the stairs filled with broken glass. 


Back in the cellar we dicovered a hidden door. 
Behind the door was a chest, and in the chest was a book and a box.
The book was written in sylvan. 
One part stood oout to me, a picture of thee gems and a text as follows:

*The gems binds the light of the morning lord to the soil. 
Without them the veins wither.
With even one theu endure*

In the box were a ring decorated with three vines.
It appeared to make the wearer more charismatic. 

Another passage that seemed very important for us was a picture of a wine that seemed like it was glowing. 
Unfortunately the text here was in a different language, one none of us could read.
